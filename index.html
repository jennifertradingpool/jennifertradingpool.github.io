ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Jennifer Welsh

I bought my first crypto in 2008. The market had crashed, and my dad, a broker at the time, encouraged me to start. I loved buying crypto and watching them move. But I didn’t have a strategy, and I was terrible with money. So I ended up selling my crypto when I needed money. Had I held on to those early investments, I’d be sitting on a pile of money today. But I wasn’t thinking long-term. I have a lot of regrets about that. It wasn’t long before I fired my financial advisor. It was clear my accounts hadn’t been managed with my best interest in mind.
